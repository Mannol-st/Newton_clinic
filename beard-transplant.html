Beard Transplant

Precision follicle placement for natural, dense facial hair.

Overview

What Is a Beard Transplant?

Beard transplantation in Coimbatore involves transplanting healthy scalp follicles to areas with sparse or no beard, placed at the correct angle and density for a natural look. At The Newton Clinic, we offer Standard Beard Transplant for mild thinning or patchy growth and Percutaneous Beard Transplant for defined, high-density styles.

  • Healthy scalp follicles are moved to sparse beard areas.
  • Angles and density are planned for a natural facial-hair pattern.
  • Suitable for both standard and high-density beard designs.

Conditions

What Conditions Does This Treatment Help With?

Patchy Beard Growth

Helpful for genetic or hormonal patchiness that affects beard coverage.

Facial Scars with Hair Loss

Can improve coverage where scars have left the beard area sparse.

Beard Density Enhancement

Useful when you want a fuller, more even beard appearance.

Gender Affirmation Surgery Support (FTM)

Supports FTM patients seeking a more masculine facial-hair profile.

Advantages

Why Choose a Beard Transplant?

Minimally Invasive

Pain-Free Procedure

Carried out under local anaesthesia for maximum comfort.

Style It Your Way

Once matured, you can shave, trim, and shape your beard as usual.
