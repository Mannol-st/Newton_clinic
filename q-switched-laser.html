What Exactly is Q-Switched Laser Therapy?

Q-Switched Laser Therapy in Coimbatore is a blistleptic treatment utilized to heal skin diseases, including pigment disease, melasma, and sunlight spots and tattoos.

High-intensity light trains which kill the melanin without killing the tissue skin by them are used during the therapy.

It not only removes skin marks, but it will also stimulate collagen, tightening skin texture and increasing the elasticity. It's also one of the most sought-after treatments for ridding you of pores, lines, wrinkles and acne marks.
