Botox in vaginismus

Botox is injected into the vaginal muscles to relax involuntary spasms making vaginal penetration & gynaecological exams painful or impossible. It is a safe, effective treatment that provides relief and allows normal intimate function to resume.

PDO Threads- vaginal application and body contouring

PDO threads are fine, dissolvable threads inserted under the skin to lift, tighten & stimulate collagen. In vaginal application, they rejuvenate & tighten the vaginal canal. On the body, they contour & firm sagging skin.

  • Women looking for non-surgical vaginal tightening or skin lifting and firming on areas like the abdomen, thighs or arms.

Carboxy therapy- stretch mark reduction

Carbon dioxide gas is injected just below the skin to boost circulation, stimulate collagen & break down scar tissue. This improves the appearance of stretch marks, making them lighter, smoother & less visible.

  • Anyone with stretch marks from pregnancy, weight gain or weight loss who wants a non-surgical way to reduce their stretch marks.

PRP-Vulvo vaginal rejuvenation and O shot

Platelet rich plasma (PRP) is taken from your own blood & injected into the vaginal & clitoral area to stimulate tissue regeneration, improve sensitivity & overall improvement of sexual health. The O-Shot is a specific PRP procedure for female sexual enhancement.

CO2 laser and RF-Genital rejuvenation

CO2 laser & radiofrequency (RF) energy are used to resurface, tighten & rejuvenate the vaginal & vulval tissues. This improves elasticity, reduces dryness and restores a more youthful appearance & function without surgery.

Prp,lasers-Non surgical management of urine incontinence

A combination of PRP injections & laser energy is used to strengthen & regenerate the tissues around the urethra and pelvic floor, reducing urine leakage without the need for surgery or medications.

The Emsella chair uses high intensity focused electromagnetic (HIFEM) technology to deliver thousands of deep pelvic floor muscle contractions in a single session equivalent to thousands of exercises. It strengthens the pelvic floor & reduces urinary leakage.
