Face PRP

Face PRP, or the Vampire Facial, is a holistic and advanced skin renewal procedure.

Overview

What Is Face PRP?

Instead of resorting to artificial chemicals or surgery, PRP takes advantage of the body's own healing ability. By drawing off a small vial of blood and filtering out its platelet-rich plasma, we can put back into your skin this concentrated factor of healing.

This is nothing less than a revolution: quicker collagen production, better skin texture, smoother finish, and healthy, radiant complexion that feels and looks like you, but better.
