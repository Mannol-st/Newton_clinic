Eyebrow Transplant

Restore fuller, natural-looking brows with precise follicle placement.

Overview

What is an Eyebrow Transplant?

Eyebrow transplant in Coimbatore is a safe, minimally invasive procedure that restores thinning or missing brows by transplanting your own healthy hair follicles. Ideal for genetic thinning, over-plucking, or scar-related hair loss, it creates fuller, natural-looking eyebrows without the need for daily makeup.

  • Your own follicles are used for a natural, lasting result.
  • The placement is designed to match the natural brow shape.
  • No daily makeup routine is needed to maintain definition.

Conditions

What Conditions Does This Treatment Help With?

Naturally sparse or thin eyebrows

Restores definition and density for brows that have always been naturally light.

Overplucking or excessive threading

Helpful for brows that have thinned over time due to repeated grooming.

Eyebrow scarring due to injury, burns, or surgery

Can fill scarred areas where natural brow hair no longer grows well.

Chemotherapy-induced hair loss

Supports brow restoration after treatment-related hair loss when appropriate.

Advantages

Why Choose an Eyebrow Transplant?

Permanent Hair Growth

Once implanted, the follicles behave like natural brows and grow hair for life.

Thicker, Fuller Brows - No Makeup Needed

Say goodbye to filling your brows daily and wake up with naturally bold, defined arches.

Minimally Invasive & Scar-Free

No stitches, no visible scarring, just elegant results with minimal pain and downtime.
