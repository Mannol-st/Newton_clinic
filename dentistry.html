Isolation And Rubber Dam

A thin rubber sheet placed in the mouth during dental procedures to keep the treatment area clean, dry and free from saliva. It improves precision and safety during fillings, root canal and bonding procedures.

  • Patients undergoing fillings, root canal treatment, bonding or whitening where a clean, dry field is essential for the best result.

Bleaching

A professional teeth whitening treatment that uses a safe bleaching agent to remove deep stains and discolouration, brightening your smile in a single or a few sessions.

  • Anyone with stained, yellowed or discoloured teeth who wants a brighter, whiter smile quickly and safely.
  • Book an appointment to discuss the best option for you

Composite Restoration

A tooth coloured resin material is carefully shaped and bonded to a damaged or decayed tooth to restore its natural look and function. Composite restorations are virtually invisible and completed in a single visit.

  • Patients with chipped, cracked, decayed or discoloured teeth who want a natural looking repair without metal fillings.
  • Book an appointment to discuss the best option for you
Book an Appointment

Diastema Closure

A cosmetic dental procedure that closes gaps between teeth, most commonly between the two front teeth using composite bonding, veneers or orthodontic treatment for a more uniform, confident smile.

  • Anyone with noticeable gaps between their teeth who wants to improve the appearance of their smile without lengthy orthodontic treatment.
  • Book an appointment to discuss the best option for you
Book an Appointment

Ellis Fracture Repair

A targeted treatment for broken or fractured teeth classified by the Ellis system based on severity. Depending on the fracture type, repair may involve bonding, crowns or root canal treatment to restore the tooth fully.

  • Patients who have chipped or fractured a tooth due to trauma, accident or biting on something hard.
  • Book an appointment to discuss the best option for you
Book an Appointment

Inlays And Onlays

Custom made restorations crafted from porcelain or composite that fit precisely within or over a damaged tooth. Inlays fill within the tooth cusps, while onlays cover one or more cusps, both providing a strong and durable alternative to regular filling.

  • Patients with moderately damaged or decayed teeth that are too large for a filling but do not require a full crown.
  • Book an appointment to discuss the best option for you
Book an Appointment

Dental Laminates / Veneers

Thin shells of porcelain or composite are custom made & bonded to the front surface of teeth to improve their shape, size, colour & overall appearance.

  • Anyone unhappy with the colour, shape, size or spacing of their teeth who wants a dramatic smile improvement with minimal tooth removal.
  • Book an appointment to discuss the best option for you
Book an Appointment

Componeers Lumineers

Componeers are prefabricated composite enamel shells that are bonded directly to teeth for quick smile improvement. Lumineers are ultra-thin porcelain veneers that require little to no tooth preparation. Both offering a fast, minimally invasive smile makeover.

  • People who want a fast, minimally invasive smile enhancement without extensive tooth preparation or multiple lengthy appointments.
Book an Appointment

Digital Smile Designing

An advanced digital process where a high-tech imaging software shows you exactly how your new smile will look before any treatment begins. This allows you & your dentist to plan & preview cosmetic changes with complete precision & confidence.

  • Anyone planning a smile makeover who wants to visualise and approve their results before committing to any dental procedure.
Book an Appointment

Tooth Jewellery

A small, safe & decorative gem or crystal is bonded to the surface of a tooth for a unique, stylish smile accent. It is completely reversible & does not damage the tooth enamel.

  • Anyone who wants to add a fun, fashionable sparkle to their smile with a safe, non-permanent decoration.
Book an Appointment

Laser Dentistry

Dental lasers are used for a wide range of treatments from gum reshaping & cavity removal to teeth whitening & treating mouth ulcers. Laser dentistry is more precise, less painful & often requires no anaesthesia.

  • People who want a more comfortable, minimally invasive dental experience with faster healing & less fear.
Book an Appointment

Dental Photography

High quality intraoral & facial photographs are taken to document your dental condition, plan cosmetic treatments & track progress over time. It is an essential part of smile designing & cosmetic case planning.

  • All people especially those undergoing cosmetic treatment who benefit from clear visual records and before & after documentation.
Book an Appointment

Fixed Partial Denture ( Crown And Bridge)

A crown is a cap placed over a damaged tooth to restore its shape & strength. A bridge uses crowns on either side of a gap to hold an artificial tooth in place - permanently replacing missing teeth without removable dentures.

  • People with severely damaged teeth needing protection or those with one or more missing teeth who want a fixed, non-removable replacement solution.
